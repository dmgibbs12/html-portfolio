The Best Movies According to D'Andre

My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

How to Train Your Dragon

I love this movie because it reminded me of when I had my first dog when I was out on my own. We had a bond, and that was something I always cherished like what was developed throughout the movie between Hiccup and Toothless.

Captain America: Winter Soldier

This is my favorite Marvel movie that came out during Marvel's peak. it had great storytelling, mystery, and Captian America is one of my favorite super heroes of all-time. It was also cool to see Bucky return in the movie too. I also really enjoyed those fight scenes they had throughout the movie as well.

John Wick

John Wick was awesome! Even though they killed a dog in the movie (fake I know) it was a great revenge story. Also seeing the gunplay in that movie from Keanu Reeves was great as well.
